Porsche Panamera Turbo S 2021

Overview

Originally thought to be too big of a departure for the storied sports car brand, the Panamera Turbo launched Porsche into the luxury sedan marketplace—allowing its high-performance ethos to reach a new audience. The 2021 Panamera Turbo S and Turbo S E-Hybrid take the speed-focused super-sedan into a new era with 620- and 689-hp powertrains, respectively. Although not quite as agile as Porsche's sports cars, the Panamera Turbo S range holds its own against rival sedans such as the Audi RS7, the BMW M5, and the Mercedes-AMG GT63. Its interior is nicely trimmed and well equipped—although the best features cost much extra to add on—and space is plentiful, especially in the long-wheelbase Executive models. Cargo space is generous too, as the Panamera cleverly hides a large cargo bay beneath its sloped hatchback. Looking for even more practicality? Consider the wagon-bodied Panamera Sport Turismo models, which we review separately.
